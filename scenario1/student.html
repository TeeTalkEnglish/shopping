Game 1 - Grocery Shopping

Situation

You just arrived in Australia and need groceries for the week.

Shopping List

Done Item Problem Hard Mode
Bread Wholegrain bread is more expensive. Ask staff for cheaper bread options.
Milk You accidentally picked almond milk. Ask where normal milk is.
Rice Rice only comes in large bags. Ask if there is a smaller size.
Chicken Chicken is almost over budget. Ask about discounts or cheaper meat.
Snacks Special price ended yesterday. Ask why shelf price is different.

Budget: $35 AUD

Your Goals

    Your Goals

    1. Ask where items are
      Show Hint
      “Excuse me, where can I find the bread?”
    2. Ask for discounts
      Show Hint
      “Is this on special?”

      “Do you have a cheaper one?”
    3. Understand fast English
      Show Hint
      “Sorry, could you say that again?”
    4. Fix one shopping mistake
      Show Hint
      “Sorry, I picked the wrong one.”

      “Can I change this?”
    5. Pay confidently
      Show Hint
      “Can I pay by card?”
