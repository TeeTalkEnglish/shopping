Game 2 - Kmart Apartment Setup

Situation

You just moved into a new apartment in Australia and need to buy affordable home items from Kmart. You must ask questions, compare products, stay on budget, and solve shopping problems naturally.

What is Kmart?

Kmart is a very popular discount department store in Australia. People often shop there for affordable apartment and home setup items.

You can buy:

  • Homewares
  • Bedding
  • Kitchen items
  • Storage boxes
  • Small appliances

Kmart is NOT a full grocery supermarket like Coles, Woolworths or Aldi.

Shopping Vocabulary

Vocabulary Meaning Example
Homewares Items for decorating or using in a home "I need affordable homewares."
Bedding Items used on a bed "Where is the bedding section?"
Storage Crates Boxes used for organizing items "Do you sell storage crates?"
Small Appliance Small electrical machine "How much is this appliance?"
Warranty Store guarantee for a product "Does this come with warranty?"
