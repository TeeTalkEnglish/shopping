Game 2 - Kmart Apartment Setup

Situation

You just moved into a new apartment and are shopping at Kmart to set up your place. You need key items, a good deal, and the right size for everything.

What is Kmart?

Kmart is a very popular discount department store in Australia. It is a common place to buy affordable homewares, bedding, kitchen items, storage boxes and small appliances.

Kmart is not a full grocery store like Coles, Woolworths or Aldi. You may find some kitchen basics, but you should go to a supermarket for fresh food, milk, bread and vegetables.

If you are unsure, ask the shop assistant whether Kmart is the right place for your apartment setup or if you should still go to a supermarket for groceries.
