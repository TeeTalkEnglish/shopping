Meat Factory Work Gear Shopping

Realistic Australian work gear shopping roleplay for new meat factory workers

Situation

You just got your first meat processing job in Australia. You start work next Monday at 5:30am.

The factory is cold, noisy, slippery, and fast-paced. Your supervisor said you MUST buy proper work gear before your first shift.

You only have $320 AUD until payday. You live in a small share house with limited storage space. You don't own a car, so delivery timing matters. You also get cold easily and cheap boots hurt your feet.

Real-life pressure: cold room + hygiene + safety + budget + comfort

Goals

  • Stay under budget
  • Buy proper safety gear before Monday
  • Use Australian English naturally
  • Ask follow-up questions confidently
  • Compare quality vs price carefully
  • Avoid slippery unsafe products
  • Understand fast workplace English
  • Handle delivery and sizing problems

Useful Vocabulary

Word Meaning Example
