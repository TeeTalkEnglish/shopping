Shop Assistant Roleplay Guide

Use fast realistic Australian retail English. Create pressure, misunderstandings, and difficult decisions.

Roleplay Instructions

  • Speak naturally and sometimes quickly
  • Recommend expensive upgrades
  • Interrupt occasionally like real retail staff
  • Create realistic misunderstandings
  • Make the student compare products carefully
  • Ask follow-up questions
  • Mention hidden costs and delivery delays
  • Use Australian slang naturally
  • Push convenience vs budget decisions
  • Force the student to explain their needs clearly
