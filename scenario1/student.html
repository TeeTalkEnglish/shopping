Game 1 - Grocery Shopping

Situation

You just arrived in Australia and need groceries for the week. You must ask supermarket staff for help, compare prices, fix shopping mistakes, and stay within your budget.

Shopping Challenge

Some products are confusing, expensive, or in the wrong aisle. Staff may speak quickly and use casual Australian English.

You should ask questions, compare products, and make decisions carefully to stay under budget.

Secret Missions

  • Stay under your $35 budget.
  • Ask for one cheaper option.
  • Fix one shopping mistake confidently.
  • Ask staff to repeat one sentence slowly.
  • Compare two products before choosing.
